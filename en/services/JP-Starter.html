FAQ - Ready for JP?

What you need to know about a career with JP

How much time do I need to invest?

The more, the better.

The equation is straightforward - the more time you can invest, the more content you can create. We use this content for your marketing, which leads to higher revenue.

You should plan to spend at least 2-3 hours per day producing content. This allows us to optimally support you in becoming successful on OnlyFans.

How long before I see results?

One of the most common questions we get from creators, but there's no one-size-fits-all answer. It depends on your current situation:

Do you already have a social media following, or are you starting from zero? How much time can you invest daily?

The more you have in place already, the faster you'll see results. But even starting from scratch, you can achieve initial results in the first few weeks.

If you're beginning from the ground up, our goal is to help you reach five-figure earnings within your first 90 days. We've successfully accomplished this multiple times with our creators.

What's the contract duration?

None! At JP Management, there's no minimum contract term. You can leave anytime you want. We believe in collaboration based on conviction, not obligation!

Which social media platforms will I be active on?

We primarily focus on Instagram and TikTok since they generate the greatest reach. If you have additional time and interest, we're happy to support your expansion to YouTube, Reddit, Twitter, and other platforms. The choice is yours!

Will I remain anonymous in my home country?

Absolutely! We primarily target the US market and have developed special strategies to ensure you remain undiscovered in your home country. Your privacy is our highest priority. We'll handle the rest!

Who owns the rights to my content?

You retain all rights to your content. No copyrights are transferred to us.
